Tables

GroundworkCSS offers clean and easy-to-customize styling for tabular data.

Example usage:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>thead th cell</th>
      <td>thead td cell</td>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <th>tbody th cell</th>
      <td>tbody td cell</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Result:

table heading th cell table heading td cell
th cell td cell

Responsive Data Tables

People love to bash on tables almost as much as they love to hate old versions of IE, but there are times when you actually should use a <table> to display data. GroundworkCSS includes easily customizable table styling to help you make your otherwise-boring tabluar data look great and stay clean.
