Buttons

GroundworkCSS buttons are clean, accessible and easy to customize.

NEW: Aria role support added for buttons. Buttons can be created by adding class="button" to elements, using a <button>,<input type="button">,<input type="submit"> or <input type="reset"> tag or by adding the Aria role to any element: role="button".
